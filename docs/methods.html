An Incomplete Accounting of my Master’s Thesis Research Thus Far

Chapter 2 Methods

In each of our study watersheds we collected precipitation data using a tipping bucket rain gauge and continuous stream depth using either a capacitance rod or pressure transducer. We also measured stream discharge monthly from April to November 2021. When measuring discharge, we recorded stream stage manually in order to calibrate the sensor data (Fig. 2.1)

Figure 2.1: Left: Blue Lake 4 (a tributary to Blue Lake) in June 2021. Right: Staff plate for manual stream stage measurement
